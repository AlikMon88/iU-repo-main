About

iU is an edtech startup currently at the Proof of Concept (POC) stage. Our vision is to create a world where individuals are not discriminated against based on their position on the social hierarchy at birth. We aim to automate the learning process in three major subject areas: Math, Science, and English , aligning with the Indian schooling system. We also educate on Finance , which is usually neglected in elementary education. By utilizing advanced chatbots as teachers, we foster a friendly teacher-student dynamic, eliminating human frustrations and inconsistencies.
